Why Google Maps Shows Rubble

81% of all structures are damaged per UNOSAT satellite analysis (Oct 2025) — this matches what you see on Google Maps and Maxar satellite imagery.

The map does not mean undamaged areas were "never bombed." UNOSAT classifies damage in tiers:

  • Destroyed — 50% (123,464) — total rubble
  • Severely damaged — 7% — visible rubble
  • Moderately damaged — 14% — appears damaged on satellite
  • Possibly damaged — 10%
  • Intact — only 19% — no detectable damage on satellite

Base layer uses real satellite imagery (Esri/Maxar). Brown/red overlay = UNOSAT-confirmed destruction.
